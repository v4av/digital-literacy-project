*Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist*

 Always check the sender’s email address carefully before trusting any message
 Do not click on suspicious or unknown links
 Never share passwords, OTPs, or login credentials with anyone
 Verify internship or job offers from official company websites
 Avoid responding to messages that create urgency or panic

 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
 Use strong and unique passwords for different platforms
 Regularly update your apps and security settings

 For UPI Safety:

* Never approve unknown payment requests
* Do not share your UPI PIN with anyone

 Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
 Call Cyber Helpline: **1930** (India)
