SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS

DO’S:
1. Use strong and secure passwords.
2. Keep your profile professional and respectful.
3. Think before posting any content online.
4. Verify information before sharing.
5. Respect others' opinions and privacy.

DON’TS:
1. Do not share personal information like phone number or address.
2. Do not post offensive or inappropriate content.
3. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
4. Do not spread fake news or misinformation.
5. Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
